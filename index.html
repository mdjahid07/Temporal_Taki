Tracing Transformation: A 30-Year Journey of Urban Expansion and Shifting Centers in Taki Municipality

Temporal Urban Expansion (1993–2023) of Taki Municipality
This map showcases the dynamic urban expansion of Taki Municipality over three decades, from 1993 to 2023. It highlights the city's transformation, driven by population growth, economic changes, and infrastructure development. The visualization provides a comprehensive narrative of Taki's urbanization journey, serving as a crucial tool for urban planners, policymakers, and researchers to analyze urban growth patterns and spatial distribution.
Urban Mean Center Shifting (1993–2023) in Taki Municipality
This map explores the spatial changes in Taki Municipality by tracking the movement of the urban mean center over time using a standard ellipse. It reveals patterns of concentrated growth and dispersion, uncovering factors like demographic changes and economic shifts. By analyzing these trends, the map offers actionable insights for planning infrastructure, managing land use, and allocating resources effectively.
